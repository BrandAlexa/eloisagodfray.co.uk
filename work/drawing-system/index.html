Drawing Systems - Reflecting on the Impacts of Covid-19

Code

Software

  • Adobe XD
  • Adobe Illustrator
  • HTML, CSS, JavaScript

Objective

The brief was to create, design and implement a rule-based system that generates a series of images based on rules and themes of our choosing. I decided to base my drawing system around the concept of lockdown and the political, social and emotional impact that it has had on our lives. I wanted to create a reflective exercise that allowed the user to take a moment and look back whilst still creating a positive outcome through a piece of artwork.

Approach

I created a list of design principles that I needed to use to guide my design process. With this in mind, I then created a series of â€¦ questions. I chose this format of questions to allow the user a greater range and, in turn, create a more diverse piece of artwork. Once I created a list of questions, I coded a piece of artwork that would be responsive to the users' answers. I user-tested this on anonymous users to ensure that they did not feel pressured or judged when answering and asked them to provide some written feedback.

Outcome

The final outcome was a range of responsive artwork in response to the political, social and emotional impact the lockdown had on our lives with the use of P5.js. Although this was a small project, I was curious how this drawing system would be used by other users, so I carried out a few more user testing. I was intrigued to see the differences or correlations between users from different ages and social-economic backgrounds and how that could be represented in a piece of art. I am proud of this project as I used a small brief to learn and develop my coding skills using a new JS library.
