Objective

The objective was to create a visualisation that would show the impact the creation of the design system has had on PUMA as a company since it first started just over a year ago. The audience is employees from all over the organisation, not just within design or tech.

Approach

I first began with collecting data from the data that Figma provides, as well as talking to colleagues who work with the design system to see how it has impacted them. I also conducted some data scrapping from the Figma documentation as well as the developer's side. Once I had a range of data, I began to construct a story and ranked what data empathised this story as well as considering the user journey. I then began an iterative process of creating several options and mock-ups of visuals that I then received feedback on and refined before constructing one visualisation.

Outcome

The outcome was a visualisation of PUMA's Design System that was designed for an audience who had little or no knowledge of design systems. I, therefore, made simplified explained visuals and used motifs that were relatable to employees working in a sports e-commerce business.
